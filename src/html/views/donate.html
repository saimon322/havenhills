Domestic Violence Donations: How to Make an Impact Today

Looking to donate to domestic violence shelters near me? A one-time donation or monthly pledge of any amount helps Haven Hills continue to provide food, traditional and emergency housing, and counseling services to hundreds of individuals and families in Los Angeles. Any domestic violence shelter donations you make are essential in transforming the lives of survivors in our local community.

Please consider one of the donation amounts below:

  • $25 sponsors school supplies for our crisis shelter
  • $45 feeds a family for a day
  • $75 provides emergency transportation fare
  • $135 feeds three families for a day
  • $500 sponsors a month of child counseling

An investment in Haven Hills is a catalyst that creates a better future for someone who might just be your neighbor, colleague, employee, acquaintance, or cherished friend. Haven Hills is proud to report that more than 80% of every dollar raised is used to provide service to survivors of domestic violence, keeping families safe and empowering them to break the cycle of violence. Haven Hills is a 501(c)(3) agency, designated by the IRS, Federal Identification Number 95-3196247.

If you would like to send a check, please make the check payable to Haven Hills and mail to P.O. Box 260, Canoga Park, CA 91305. Otherwise, complete the below form to donate online

From all of us at Haven Hills, we send our deepest thanks for making a difference today.
